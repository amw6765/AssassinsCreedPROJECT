Actions vs. Speakers

In this graph we have compared the number of actions vs. the number of speakers in each chapter. By looking at this comparison we are able to get a sense of which chapters had more moving parts than others. From the outside looking in we are able to tell what chapter in the game had the most or the least amount of gameplay action taking placee.

Speeches vs. Speakers

In this graph we drew the compaarion between the number of speeches vs the number of speakers in each chapter of the game. By having this visual we are able to identify what chaapter has more or less content than another chapter in the game.
